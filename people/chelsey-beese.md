Chelsey is a **research assistant** in the MEEM lab, working on the Marsden Fund project exploring temperate mesophotic ecosystems. She uses size-spectrum models and field data to study these unique habitats. Chelsey is also a **postdoctoral scholar** with the [IMF Lab](https://cheryl-barnes.github.io/integrated-marine-fisheries.html) at Oregon State University. She has a background in marine science, applied mathematics, and ecosystem modelling. Her favourite reef fish is the mandarin dragonet (*Synchiropus splendidus*).
